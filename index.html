Numberball Simulator


Bottom of the ninth, can you be the hero?


Rules

Pick your team name and try to win the game! The pitcher will submit a pitching number from 1-100, submit your own hitting number
and try to get as close as possible to the pitching number to win! The number scale is looping, meaning guesses of 1 and 99 have
a difference of 2. If the difference between the hitting and pitching numbers is 10 or less, you win the game!
