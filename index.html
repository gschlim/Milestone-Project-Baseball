Numberball Simulator


Bottom of the ninth, can you be the hero?


Rules

Pick your team name and try to win the game! The pitcher will submit a pitching number from 1-100. Submit your own hitting number
and try to get as close as possible to the pitching number to win! The number scale is looping, so the difference will never be
higher than 50 (for example, the numbers 99 and 1 are two apart [99, 100, 1])
If the difference between the hitting and pitching numbers is 10 or less, you win the game!
